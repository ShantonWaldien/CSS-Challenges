Ballantines Scotch Whisky Live Tastings, Brand Education & Cocktail Recipes.

Ballantine's is a range of Blended Scotch whiskies produced by Pernod Ricard in Dumbarton, Scotland. The Ballantine's flavour is dependent on fingerprint malts from Miltonduff and Glenburgie, blended with fifty single malts and four single grains. The brand has won many accolades and awards for its products.
